# Executive Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary
The Tableau Executive Dashboard gives retail leadership a unified view of sales performance, profitability, customer behaviour, shipping efficiency, discount effectiveness, and product returns. It enables quick identification of business strengths, operational risks, and areas with room for improvement.

---

## What Is Performing Well

* The South region leads all others in total sales generated.
* Technology products deliver the highest profitability across all categories.
* Sales revenue is well spread across all customer segments.
* Overall sales performance has remained steady throughout the year.

---

## What Is Underperforming

* Elevated discount levels are dragging down overall profitability.
* Standard shipping continues to carry the longest average delivery times.
* Furniture products record the greatest number of returned orders.

---

## Risks

* Profit margins compress as discount levels increase.
* High return volumes drive up operational costs.
* Shipping delays risk negatively impacting customer satisfaction.

---

## Opportunities

* Expand high-performing technology products into additional regions.
* Strengthen delivery performance through logistics process improvements.
* Refine promotional discount structures to protect profitability.

---

## Recommended Business Actions

* Reduce unnecessary or excessive discounting.
* Improve supply chain and logistics efficiency.
* Track return trends on a regular basis.
* Increase investment in high-performing products and regions.

---

## Limitations
The dashboard is built on the available dataset only and should be used alongside additional operational and financial data before any major strategic decisions are made.

---

## Suggested Next Analysis
Future analytical work should explore customer lifetime value, campaign effectiveness, sales forecasting models, inventory optimisation, and regional demand forecasting.
